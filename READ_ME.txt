This app is designed to calculate the hours an individual worked on the Outlier platform using the exportable CSV file that is available in the 'Earnings' tab.

Instructions: 
1. Go to the 'Earnings' page on the Outlier platform.
2. Click on the earnings report that you want to know the hours for.
3. Select 'Download CSV' at the top right of the earnings report.
4. Upload the downloaded file using the 'Upload CSV' button to find out your total hours.
